## What is ErgoCity?

ErgoCity is an educational platform that teaches you about **ergodicity** - a mathematical concept that shows how individual experiences can differ from group averages in certain situations.

### The Core Concept

Imagine you and 100 friends each start with $1,000 and play a game where:
- You have a 60% chance to win and gain money
- You have a 40% chance to lose and lose money

**Here's the fascinating part:**
- If you look at all 100 people after one round (the "ensemble average"), on average they might have more money
- But if you look at one person playing 100 rounds (the "time average"), they might end up with less money

This is the **ergodic paradox** - and ErgoCity lets you explore this concept hands-on!

### How Scoring Works

The game uses **multiplicative** changes to your score:

**Example with LOW risk (starting with 1,000 units):**

**If you WIN (55% chance):**
- Your score increases by 29.1%

**If you LOSE (45% chance):**
- Your score decreases by 30.0%

- You lost 300 units

> **Important:** Each round multiplies your current score, so your gains and losses compound over time!

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### "Please connect your wallet to interact"
**Solution:** Click the wallet connection button and approve the connection request in your wallet.

#### "Insufficient MATIC for gas"
**Solution:** You need more MATIC in your wallet for transaction fees. Purchase a small amount through your wallet or a cryptocurrency exchange.

#### "Incorrect Network. Please switch to target network"
**Solution:** Your wallet is connected to the wrong blockchain network. Switch to Polygon (MATIC) network in your wallet settings.

#### "Contract not found at configured address"
**Solution:** The blockchain contract may not be deployed on the network you're connected to. Verify you're on Polygon network.

#### "Score is zero. Reset to play"
**Solution:** Your blockchain score has reached zero. Click "Reset Score" to return to 1,000 units.

#### "Already registered with a score"
**Solution:** You're already registered! You can start playing immediately using "Engage ErgoDrive!"

#### Transaction is Pending for a Long Time
**Solution:** Blockchain transactions can take 10-60 seconds during high network activity. Be patient and don't submit multiple transactions.

#### MetaMask Not Showing Up
**Solution:** 
1. Ensure MetaMask is installed
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Check if MetaMask is unlocked
4. Try disconnecting and reconnecting

## Glossary of Terms

### General Terms

**ErgoCity**: The name of this application - a play on "ergodicity" and "city"

**Ergodicity**: A mathematical property where the time average equals the ensemble average. This app demonstrates a NON-ergodic system where they differ.

**Units**: The scoring currency used in the game (not real money in simulation mode)

### Game Terms

**Score/Balance**: Your current number of units (starts at 1,000)

**Risk Protocol**: The rule set you choose for each round, determining win probability and payout multipliers

**Success Rate**: The percentage chance you'll win the round (e.g., 55% means you win 55 out of 100 times on average)

**Win Bonus**: The percentage your score increases if you win (e.g., +29.1% means your score becomes 129.1% of what it was)

**Loss Penalty**: The percentage your score decreases if you lose (e.g., -30.0% means your score becomes 70% of what it was)

### Statistical Terms

**Ensemble Average**: The average score across ALL players at a single point in time

**Time Average**: The average score of ONE player over many rounds

**Multiplicative Dynamics**: Each round multiplies your score by a factor (1.291 for a win, 0.70 for a loss in LOW risk)

**Non-Ergodic System**: A system where individual long-term outcomes differ from group averages

### Blockchain Terms

**Wallet**: Software that stores your cryptocurrency and lets you interact with blockchain applications

**MetaMask**: A popular browser extension cryptocurrency wallet

**WalletConnect**: A protocol that lets you connect mobile wallets to web applications

**MATIC**: The cryptocurrency used on the Polygon network for transaction fees

**Gas Fees**: Small transaction fees paid in MATIC to process blockchain operations

**Polygon Network**: The blockchain network ErgoCity uses (faster and cheaper than Ethereum)

**Transaction**: An operation on the blockchain (like registering, playing, or resetting)

**Confirmation**: When your transaction is successfully added to the blockchain

**Smart Contract**: The blockchain program that runs ErgoCity's game logic

### Interface Terms

**The Grid**: The blockchain network (a reference to the movie TRON)

**ErgoDrive**: The game engine/mechanism (playing on blockchain)

**Grid Engagement**: Playing on the blockchain

**Simulated Grid**: Playing in local simulation mode

**Quantum Protocol**: The randomness algorithm (sounds cooler than "random number generator"!)

## Tips for Understanding Ergodicity

1. **Play Many Rounds in Simulation**
   - Play 20-50 rounds and track your score
   - Notice how it tends to decrease over time, even with "good odds"

2. **Compare to Ensemble Average**
   - Look at the Ensemble Average (all blockchain players)
   - Compare it to your individual journey
   - They're often different - that's the ergodic paradox!

3. **Experiment with Different Protocols**
   - Try LOW risk for 20 rounds
   - Reset and try HIGH risk for 20 rounds
   - See which strategy works better long-term

4. **Understand Compounding**
   - Winning 3 times: 1000 √ó 1.291 √ó 1.291 √ó 1.291 = 2,152
   - Losing 3 times: 1000 √ó 0.70 √ó 0.70 √ó 0.70 = 343
   - Even good odds can lead to losses with multiplication!
