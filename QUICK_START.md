### Option 2: Try Blockchain Mode (Advanced) üîó

**Perfect for:** Crypto users, those wanting permanent records

**Prerequisites:**
- MetaMask or compatible wallet installed
- Small amount of MATIC (‚âà$1-2 worth)
- 5-10 minutes for setup

**Steps:**

#### 1. Get a Wallet (if you don't have one)
- Install [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/) browser extension
- Create a new wallet (save your seed phrase safely!)
- Or use your existing wallet

#### 2. Get MATIC
- Purchase on Coinbase, Binance, or another exchange
- Send to your wallet address
- Or use a faucet for small test amounts

#### 3. Switch to Polygon Network
In MetaMask:
- Click network dropdown (top of MetaMask)
- Select "Polygon Mainnet"
- (If not listed, add it manually with Chain ID: 137)

#### 4. Connect to ErgoCity
- Find the wallet button (usually in sidebar or top corner)
- Click "Connect MetaMask" or "Connect Wallet"
- Approve the connection request

#### 5. Register Your Identity
- Look for the purple "Engage ErgoDrive" box
- Click "Register Identity"
- Approve the transaction in your wallet
- Wait 5-15 seconds for confirmation

#### 6. Play!
- Select your risk protocol from the dropdown
- Click "Engage ErgoDrive!"
- Approve the transaction
- Wait for your result
- Your score is now on the blockchain!

## üìä Understanding Your Screen

### Top Section: Score Displays

**Current (Simulated) Balance**
- Your score in simulation mode
- Starts at 1,000 units
- Ephemeral (resets when you refresh)

**Ensemble Average (Grid)**
- Average of ALL blockchain players
- Updated in real-time
- Shows the "group average"

### Middle Section: Blockchain Engagement

**Engage ErgoDrive**
- Connect wallet here
- Register for blockchain mode
- Play on the blockchain
- See your permanent score

### Risk Protocol Cards

Each card shows:
- **Risk Level**: LOW to MAXIMUM
- **Success Rate**: % chance to win (55% to 80%)
- **Win Bonus**: How much you gain if you win
- **Loss Penalty**: How much you lose if you lose

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Playing without understanding**
   - Read the Ergodic Paradox explanation on the page
   - Try simulation mode first!

2. **Choosing high risk too early**
   - Start with LOW or MILD risk
   - Understand the mechanics before going extreme

3. **Not reading transaction confirmations**
   - Always check your wallet before approving
   - Verify the transaction fee is reasonable

4. **Expecting to "win" long-term**
   - This is educational, not a money-maker
   - Even with good odds, you can lose over time
   - That's the whole point of the ergodicity lesson!

5. **Wrong network** (Blockchain mode)
   - Make sure you're on Polygon, not Ethereum
   - Check your wallet's network selector
